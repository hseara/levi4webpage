About Levi IV

To keep with the tradition of our productive and socially vivid scientific meetings (Levi 2013, Nove Hrady 2015, and Dresden 2016) that were born from Ilpo Vattulainen and Pavel Jungwirth collaboration, we are organizing a new meeting (Levi IV) in Nove Hrady on May 23-26, 2018. This will be kept in the style of a small conference encouraging direct communication in a friendly atmosphere. The topic as before focuses mostly on "Membranes and monolayers and any related process" covering both experimental and computational aspects.

Program

Thirteen thematic scientific sessions are planned during the four conference days. More datais will be announced soon.
